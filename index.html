The defining challenge of the AI era is not deploying agents.

It is continuously converting human capability into organizational capability.

My work focuses on understanding how expertise moves through organizations—from human knowledge, to skills, to agents, to scalable business capabilities.

Throughout my career I have worked across infrastructure, cloud, enterprise architecture, AI platforms, and emerging technology strategy. These experiences increasingly point to the same conclusion: organizations that learn fastest will outperform organizations that simply automate fastest.

I am currently developing frameworks around Capability Engineering, Human-Hybrid Operating Models, enterprise AI governance, and progressive autonomy to help organizations build systems that continuously capture, refine, and scale expertise.

This site is where I publish those ideas, frameworks, and ongoing research.
